About Maxmart Trading

Maxmart is a sister concern of MAAM Trading based in Kingdom of Bahrain. It is a flagship venture introduced by a group of professionals who are experts in diversified field of business. The business house comprises of the corporate office, exclusive production unit, extensive warehouse and a showroom which will be our retail outlet.

Maxmart trading has diversified business operations namely Corporate Uniforms, Garments, Corporate Tissues and Personalized Protective Equipments.

1. Corporate Uniforms: Maxmart’s strong hold! We boast about an exclusive uniforms production unit in Bahrain, which is fully equipped with sophisticated and state-of-the-art machineries and highly skilled man power. Our team of professionals can produce any type of uniforms used in corporate offices, Hotels, Hospitals, Manufacturing plants, construction houses etc.

2. Garments:Our retail outlet has an exclusive collection of premium quality designer garments ranging from Shirts, Trousers, T-Shirts, Winter Collections etc.

3. Corporate Tissues:Maxmart trading maintain stocks of various types of Tissues required for corporate houses, hospitality industry, Hospitals etc., We are capable to Brand the tissues ranging from box tissues to napkins, based on client requirement.

4. PPE:Maxmart Trading also deals with Personal Protective Equipments like Coverals, Helmets, Safety Shoes etc., of high quality.
