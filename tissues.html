Corporate Tissues

Nice Tissue Paper Industry LLC has rapidly become one of the leading privately owned tissue converters in the Middle East. With over Two years of experience in all levels of manufacturing and management Nice Tissue Paper industry has the quality and the commitment to provide top quality products at competitive prices, with exceptional service. State of the art machinery has been installed to give the company the ability to compete successfully in all segments of trade for which it produces products. Stringent procedures are adhered to in order to maintain product quality and manufacturing efficiencies.

All the products produced by Nice Tissue Paper industry, in its various brands are also available for Private Label production and distribution. Major retail and commercial customers are trusting Nice Tissue Paper Industry to produce and distribute their own store brands of paper product lines.

Nice Tissue Paper Industry, manufactures a full line of paper products for the hospitality, food service segment, Janitorial, Facilities Management & Cleaning, Airlines and institutional segments of business throughout the Middle East region. Nice Tissue Paper Industry also supplies institutions with a full dispenser solutions offering from Plain White, Clear, Smoke transparent or customized color and Art design dispensers. The dispensers are designed and manufactured for high traffic usage from high quality materials and components.

The entity has overcome significant challenges during the period and continues its core business performance in the field. The present production is 100 metric ton per month, which will soon be increased to 500 metric tons.
